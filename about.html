About us

Our mission is to provide free, quality and accessible online tutoring to all elementary and middle school students regardless of their economic and immigration status, race, sex, etc. in the Greater Boston area.

Why BCT?

Boston COVID-Tutoring keeps students engaged in their academics and helps ensure that they are fully grasping the material they are responsible for learning. Our tremendous group of tutors consists of some of the finest students from top schools in the Greater Boston area. Each lesson is carefully designed to challenge and interest students to make learning both stimulating and enjoyable.

Boston COVID-Tutoring (BCT) was founded by high school students from the Buckingham Browne & Nichols School in Cambridge, MA in response to the COVID-19 pandemic. We provide free online tutoring to students at the elementary, middle school, and high school levels to help fill in the academic gaps created by the disruption of traditional schooling. We welcome all students, regardless of their background (race, sex, immigrant status, etc.) to work with us in pursuit of their academic goals. BCT recognizes that all children have the right and deserve the opportunity to expand their knowledge and pursue their academic passions. Knowledge is the most powerful and important tool that impacts one's entire life from choosing a career path to pursuing an academic passion.

Philosophy

Boston COVID-Tutoring was founded on the premise that learning is essential for success but also should be rewarding. Unfortunately, for many of us, COVID-19 has created obstacles to learning. Boston COVID-Tutoring attempts to address the resulting deficits. Our goal is to engage students, create opportunities for addressing academic shortcomings, and spark students' curiosity to make them lifelong learners.
